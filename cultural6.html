Anticipated Effects

The effects of research and education, if done in a proper way, would be in my opinion beneficial to those whose lives are directly affected by classism. Research on classism would help broaden knowledge about the social issue, to understand truly how it works and why people develop that kind of prejudice and stigma towards people of a lower social status. The knowledge gained through classism could then be applied in the education step of the solution process. In fact, once researchers and specialists know with more accuracy what classism is, then they can help other people understand it too through education. Thanks to education more and more would be able to recognize the issue and hopefully they would know how to react, in order to intervene in time.
