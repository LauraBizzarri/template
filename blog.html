Blog entry 1

The purpose of the video was to show people, and in particular people within the gay community, the hardships that many gay people have to face. To do this the author used a personal letter addressed to his uncle. The video starts out showing the letter the author wrote to his uncle and then the author himself appears to recite the letter. I think that this structure helped the author get the attention of the audience. In fact, I think that using a personal letter is avery effective way to address such a topic. If for example, the author only stated his opinion the audience might have wandered how he knows what he's saying. However, talking aboout his personal experience first amd then eventually getting to making broader statements addressed to people in his same situation, is a better way to make the audience interested. The video is actually very helpful, becuase it shows a way in which an effective narrative could be structured, to make it more personal but also address a broader topic.

Blog entry 2

The target audience of the video is people whose lives are greatly affected by video games or other types of technology. The social issue depicted in this episode of South Park, is that nowadays many people spend a lot of their time video gaming. This can become a serious problem as more and more people are attracted to video games and get addicted to them. In fact, just like addictive drugs can have harmful effects on people on the long run, so do video games. If someone spends too much time playing them, the risk is that they will end up separating themselves from reality, and not having any type of social interactions, outside of the game. Social interactions, even though many people don't realize it, are a very important part of people's lives, and help them maintain mental as well as physical stability. I think, that the bad effects that too much video gaming can have on people is mainly what the creators of the show were trying to portray. In fact, throughout the episode when they referred to players who had achieved higher levels in the game, and had therefore played for a longer time, as people with 'no life'. This means that those people spent so much time on the video game that they ended up being completely detached from reality and deprived of any social interaction. This, in my opinion, was the creators' view on this particular social issue.

Blog entry 4

We were shown an Chrysler commercial which featured the city of Detroit as well as the popular rapper Eminem. I think that the imagery of the city of Detroit was one of city that has hard work at its soul, and that has been through hard times but has always been able to get itself out of it. Even though it is known to many people that the city of Detroit has been through really rough times, I think that this advertisement was able to depict Detroit in a better light then it usually is in other situations. The use of Eminem as a celebrity representative for the motor company I think helped to increase the effectiveness of the commercial. In fact, many people might be more drawn to buy that car or another model of the same brand because they saw a famous rapper in the commercial. This might be an unconscious process but it's an effective one, as its wide use in advertisements shows. The narrator's voice that was describing the city of Detroit as a city that's been able to stand back up after rough times, I think was another very effective way of drawing the audience's attention. In fact, what the voice says, the tone of it, as well as some of the 'challenging questions' it says, are ways to make the viewer feel more involved and drawn towards the advertised car. I think that because of all these techniques the ad achieved its purpose of making the audience more interested in their product.
