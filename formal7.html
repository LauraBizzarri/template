Conclusion

In conclusion, research, communication and technology are all major factors in Entomology. They all play different roles but they are ultimately interrelated. Research provides a basis on which the knowledge within Entomology builds, based on the results that researchers get from experiments. Communication, in all its forms, allows entomologists to share ideas and information with each other, as well as with the public. Finally, technology is a powerful way to make work within Entomology more efficient, whether by making communication faster or research more reliable.
